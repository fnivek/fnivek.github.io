Acshi Haggenmiller

I am a PhD student at the University of Michigan in Edwin Olson's APRIL Robotics Laboratory. I have designed hardware and algorithms for ultra-wideband radios to perform graph realization and localization. I have improved the precision and speed of the AprilTags visual fiducial system. I have worked on monte-carlo tree search methods for probabilistic capture of adversarial targets. I have collaborated on sponsored projects including research on the automation of rural mail delivery routes for the US Postal Service.
